A quick directory of reliable (and not-so-reliable) places to eat, drink, gear up, file paperwork, and overhear something you weren’t meant to.

## Taverns & Tea Houses
- [[The Bent Nail Taproom]] — loud drinks, louder stories, and tabs paid in questionable scrip
- [[Oaths n' Oars]] — riverfront warmth, rescue-crew regulars, and boats that arrive wrong
- [[The Painted Buckle]] — tea-house calm with knife-edge gossip and private booths
- [[The Dewmist Driftway]] — last stop before the gates, cheap beds, careful conversations

## Shops & Services
- [[Ribbon & Ration]] — outfitter kits, rope, rations, and "guarantees" with fine print
- [[Brassleaf Curios & Repairs]] — emergency repairs, odd enchantments, and one humming cabinet
- [[Ink & Iron Registry]] — contracts, waivers, charters, and paperwork that can kill you slower than a dungeon
- [[Hinge & Harness Stableworks]] — stables, tack, wheel repair, and discreet storage

- [[Flagon Alchemy]] — fast-brew potions, tonics, and “field-stable” mixes
- [[Buckleforge Blades & Bucklers]] — fitted steel, balanced weapons, and strapwork that holds
- [[Candlewisp & Cord]] — rope, lanterns, hooks, and rigging for bad air and worse drops
- [[Whetstone Ward]] — minor runes, wards, whetstones, and gear protection
- [[Patches Field Surgeonry]] — stitching, salves, splints, and post-dungeon care
- [[The Cartographer's Corner]] — maps, prints, and “approved” routes that may be lies

## Transportation
- [[Dewmist Lantern Ferries]] — crossings on [[The Dewmist River]] using lantern code and fog-whistles
- [[The Path-to-Adventure Coachline]] — southern road carriages via [[The Path to Adventure]]
- [[Amorpha Pass Haul & Sledge]] — northern freight and winter travel via [[Amorpha Pass]]
