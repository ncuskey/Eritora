"We know the fog. The fog knows us."

**Owner:** [[Nella Quay]]
**Location:** [[South Docks]] on [[The Dewmist River]], [[Turnbuckle Town]]
**Number in Employ:** 6 guides, 12 boat crew, 3 runners
**Known For:** River-route guiding, fog navigation, and quiet entry into town

Lanternline Wayfinders guide clients through the parts of [[Turnbuckle Town]] the brochures don’t mention—docks, fog, and the subtle currents that move people and goods without applause. They’re less about glory and more about arrival, timing, and clean exits when something feels wrong.

If you want a guide who reads weather like a warning and crowds like a trap, you hire Lanternline.
